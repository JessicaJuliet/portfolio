Store Page Redevelopment

Sector:

Luxury Jewellery Industry

Region:

Ireland

Date Completed:

October 2022

Requirements

To improve the UX/UI of the company's store page to aid users in finding important information quickly.

Approach

I collaborated closely with the UX team to discuss the ways in which we could enhance the user experience on one of our highly visited web pages. I developed a new visually appealing layout, bringing the most important information above the fold for users.

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, SCSS, Shopify

Challenges Faced

The challenges faced were finding ways to fit a lot of content above the fold, without overloading users or making the text size too small. This was achieved by using a grid layout, which I developed using the CSS grid.
